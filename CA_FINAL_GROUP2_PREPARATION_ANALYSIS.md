## A. Direct Tax Laws & International Taxation (DT)

### Core buckets to track

1. **Basic framework**
   - Residential status, scope of total income, income deemed to accrue/arise
2. **Heads of income**
   - Salary, house property, PGBP, capital gains, other sources
3. **Set-off / carry forward / clubbing / deductions**
4. **Assessment procedures & return related provisions**
5. **TDS/TCS, advance tax, interest, fee, penalties (high-yield practical areas)**
6. **Assessment & reassessment, appeals, rectification, revision**
7. **Search, survey, updated return, faceless assessments (conceptual + amendments)**
8. **International taxation**
   - DTAA basics, treaty override/relief concepts, transfer pricing basics, equalization levy/significant cross-border themes as applicable
9. **Recent amendments + case laws + RTP/MTP integration**

## B. Indirect Tax Laws (IDT)

### Core buckets to track

1. **GST law architecture**
   - Supply, composite/mixed supply, time/place/value of supply
2. **Input Tax Credit (ITC)**
   - Conditions, blocked credits, reversals/reclaims
3. **Registration, tax invoice, returns, payment**
4. **Job work, e-way bill, documentation compliance**
5. **Assessment, audit, inspection, demand & recovery**
6. **Refunds, exports/SEZ, LUT/Bond, inverted duty structures**
7. **Appeals, offences, penalties, prosecution (practical triggers)**
8. **Customs**
   - Import/export process, valuation, duty computation, exemptions, baggage/EOU/FTP-linked practical concepts
9. **Amendments + notifications + case law-oriented MCQs/caselets**

## C. Integrated Business Solutions (IBS)

### Core buckets to track

1. **Integrated case solving framework**
   - Identify issue, map law/standard, evaluate options, conclude with rationale
2. **Cross-domain application**
   - Mix of finance, law, tax, governance, strategy, performance, ethics in one case
3. **Data interpretation for decision-making**
   - Ratios, variance, scenario analysis, cash/profit impact logic
4. **Professional communication**
   - Structured, concise, assumption-backed answers
5. **Open-book efficiency (if applicable in your attempt pattern)**
   - Indexing notes, tabbing modules, locating standards/provisions fast
6. **Mock case drills (timed)**
   - 3-hour simulation with post-analysis
